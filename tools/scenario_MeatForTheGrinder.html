Meat for the Harvest

Scavenged Weapons

In-battle Special rules
  • All fighters start with the skills True Grit, Iron Jaw, and Unstoppable.
  • Weapons are poor quality, Ammo checks are always failed.
  • Fighters can open crates using a Loot (basic) action (no skill test required). Roll 2D6 against the table above, gain the indicated weapon, and then remove the crate.
  • If a fighter with a Scavenged weapon is Taken out of Action, the weapon is dropped at the centre of their base. Other fighters can pick it up using a Loot (basic) action.
