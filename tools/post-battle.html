The Post Battle Sequence

Immediately after the game

The rules here reflect campaign House rules.

1. Wrap-Up ("Main" game only)

A. Succumbing to Injuries

If a fighter was:

  • Prone & Seriously Injured when the Battle ended, or
  • Prone & Seriously Injured when they fled the battle (e.g. after a gang had bottled out)

Roll 1D6, on a 1 or 2 they suffer a Lasting Injury.

B. Treat Critical Injuries

C. Did Captured fighters escape?

For each friendly fighter that suffered a lasting injury of CAPTURED, test to see if they escape.

D. Ransoming Captured fighters

Fighters are always released back to their gangs after the battle. The question is whether they will have all their body parts...

  • Enhanced Guilder Bounties! A leader or champion of the Bountied Gang may be ransomed back for 50% of their credit value
  • Standard Bounties: Any other fighters can be ransomed back for 25% of their credit value.
  • Prisoner Exchange: If both gangs agree, other fighters can be exchanged on a fighter-for-fighter basis without either gang paying a ransom.

Fighters that weren't exchanged or ransomed suffer a Lasting Injury!

2. Assign / Reassign Territories ("Main" game only)

3. Receive Rewards ("Main" game only)

Guilder Bounties — Performance Bonus

If fighting the "Bountied Gang" then you can earn additional credits from ONE of the following options:

  • 100 credits if at least one Leader or Champion was killed (includes if they die during treatment for Critical Injuries).
  • 50 credits if at least one Leader or Champion went Out of Action.

The 100 / 50 credits are paid by local guilders.
