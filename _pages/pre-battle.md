# Pre Battle Sequence

| No. | Step | Details |
|-----|------|---------|
| 1 | Make a Challenge & Stake Territory | • Just organise a game. <br>• Randomise "challenger". <br>• No penalties for saying no. |
| 2 | Recruit Hired Guns | • Skip this step. |
| 3 | Determine Scenario | • Main campaign game -> Consult with the arbitrator. <br>• Otherwise... pick something fun / thematic. <br>• Use the Open Hive war pack if struggling to choose (https://openhivewar.com/) |
| 4 | Set up the Battlefield | • As normal. |
| 5 | Choose Crews | • If you are fighting the gang with a bounty on it (which is determined at the start each campaign week) you may recruit a bounty hunter for free. <br>• If you have the lower gang rating; you may choose to spend up to the difference in your gang ratings on this bounty hunter which must come from the guild. <br>• Use any reasonable model to represent it. |
| 6 | Announce Territory Boons | • As normal. |
| 7 | Gang Tactics | • As normal. |
| 8 | Deployment | • As normal. |
