# Post Battle Sequence

## Immediate	

### Guilder Bounties - Performance Bonus

If fighting the "Bountied Gang" then you can earn additional credits from ONE of the following options:
- 100 credits if at least one Leader or Champion was **killed** (includes if they die during treatment for Critical Injuries).
- 50 credits if at least one Leader or Champion went **Out of Action**.

The 100 / 50 credits are paid by local guilders.

### Guilder Bounties - Release Cost

Captured Leaders and Champions cost 50% not 25% for uninjured release

### Standard Post Battle Steps

| No. | Step | Details |
|-----|------|---------|
| 1 | Wrap Up | • Main game only - As normal. <br>• Otherwise fighters escape without issue. |
| 2 | Assign / Reassign Territory | • Main game only - As normal. <br>• Otherwise fighters escape without issue. |
| 3 | Receive Rewards | • As normal. |
| 4 | Collect Income | • Do not complete - triggered only at month end when instructed by arbitrator. |

- Claim Bounties
